About Me

Maddie P is a poet based in Austin, Texas, whose work explores the complexities of identity, mental health, love, and social justice. As the 2020 Houston Youth Poet Laureate and a 2023 TEDxUTAustin speaker, Maddie’s voice has resonated across diverse platforms, including The New York Times, where her piece “To Be a Black Girl” was featured. Her poetry delves into themes of anxiety, depression, and feminism, offering a powerful, nuanced look at the intersections of personal experience and broader societal issues. Maddie’s writing is a testament to the resilience of the human spirit, challenging norms and inviting reflection on what it means to navigate the world.
